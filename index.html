Mind Right is a clinical psychotherapy & counselling practice that specialises in the assessment and treatment of a wide range of mental health problems experienced by adults, teenagers, and children including depression and anxiety.

Psychotherapy at MindRight is tailored to the individual needs of the client with the goal of providing significant improvement in the mental health of clients within 8-12 sessions.

Longer term support is available for clients presenting with more complex needs and longstanding problems.



Mind Right Founder and Psycotherapist Jennifer has 18+ years experience in the field of mental health and social services. With 10+ of those years specialising in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy(CBT).

She is an Accredited Psychotherapist with the British Association of Behavioural and Cognitive Psychotherapy(BABCP), a Registered Counsellor with both the Australian Counselling Association and the British Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy(MCABCP).


Jennifer has a strong passion for improving access to psychological therapy for people from all walks of life. She has experience working with a broad range of client groups from, Criminal Justice systems, to Trauma & AOD. She has delivered systemic focused therapy groups to FDV woman and children recovering from domestic abuse under the CEDAR programme, and worked with suicide, life limiting illness and self harm behaviours. She has a wealth of experience in the evidenced based treatment of a range of psychological disorders from Generalised Anxiety, Panic Disorder and Depression to PTSD and Agoraphobia. In previous years she has coordinated and delivered school counselling programmes and developed main stream school teachings to include mental health and wellbeing as part of their every day curriculum as a preventative measure to our international mental health needs.


In more recent years Jennifer has been involved in passing on her knowledge, skills and expertise whilst teaching other therapists as a Course Tutor on a SCQF Level 10 Post Graduate Diploma Course in Psychotherapy and also provided Clinical Supervision and Accreditation support to other Counsellors wishing to progress in the field of therapy.


In the therapy room Jennifer can offer you a safe space to explore your life difficulties whilst suspending any judgement. Emotional wounds can be felt, validated and processed with compassion before exploring the evidence based practical solutions for your psychological treatment. Trauma informed and trained in suicide intervention, she feels a sense of privilege in supporting others to be able to re-engage in their lives without their difficulties. A holistic approach to client focused counselling supporting you to manage your mental and emotional wellbeing.

The CBT model of psychotherapy that we specialise in at MindRight is recognised internationally as the "gold standard" treatment in the management of psychological problems. It is one of the most established and researched psychological talking treatments which is considerably effective for clients presenting with acute psychological difficulties. The National Institute for Health & Care Excellence (NICE) recommends CBT as a first choice for a range of problems.
